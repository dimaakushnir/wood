Why intelligent furniture?

We look into the future and we are creating it!

Obviously, sooner or later, houses will become increasingly technological and will become places where all systems can be controlled directly via your smartphone from anywhere.
You would simply need to open an app to see the statistics and choose the most optimal and economical mode to control your systems. Furniture will not be absent here, and, even at present, many companies are thinking about taking furniture to a new level of interaction with its owners. We know that this will happen in the future, and we wont to be among those who create this future. It is a great honor and responsibility. And we do not wish to pass this responsibility onto others!
We believe that a smart house concept will become truly functional if it’s also complemented by intelligent furniture . And with “intelligent” we mean technological! This means that all interior components will connect to just one ecosystem through an application on your furniture and allowing you to fully interact with your habitat, elevating your comfort and convenience moving to a new level.
